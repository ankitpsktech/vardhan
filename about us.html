  • Why vardhan sez consultants ?

We, M/S. Vardhan Sez Consultants are knowledge-based Organization. We have a team of skilled and educated manpower. We are one of the leading custom Clearing & Forwarding Agents of India, having in House advisory and consultancy services. This Advisory and consultancy services are not provided by other custom Brokers. Along with our value added services, we are specialist in handling of Export & Import cargo for more than 4 years. We are having our custom clearance network at all major customs ports of India including Nhava Sheva, Mumbai, Sahar Air Cargo Mumbai & Arshiya FTWZ, Mundra, Hazira, Delhi and Chennai.
We, Vardhan Sez Consultants are providing integrated logistics services including custom clearance, Value Added Services and Legal support to importers & exporters, SEZ & STPI Consultancy and Customs Liasoning . This company has been established to provide all Indirect Tax related services to its clients under one roof including advisory and legal services.
We are serving our clients through our service branches located across multiple cities in India and delivering through seasoned professionals backed by knowledge and experience of its promoters. Vardhan SEZ Consultants offers “One Stop Solutions” to all SEZ & STPI related requirements of various stakeholders of SEZs & STPI’s.


Vardhan SEZ Consultants is in the corporate world with young thought. Our Mission is to create India an enclave of world’s manufacturer hub. We will promote India similar to SEZ India and achieve target of greatest share in the world trade. Creation of a strong infrastructure of knowledge super power and on line availability of information on SEZ to support business and working environment of SEZ corporation & citizen of India by creating an integrated development which is self sufficient and initiate a steady flow of new ideas technology and enhancing and creating a skilled work force to support it.
